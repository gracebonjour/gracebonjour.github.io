Hi, I'm the creator of Grace's Dog Training

I'm a hobbyist trainer who has spent two years working with a reactive cattle dog named Dino, and this site is everything I wish someone had given me on day one.

Grace's Dog Training is a small resource on balanced, e-collar-assisted training: how the tools work and a lesson plan that goes from the basic "sit", to more advanced off leash work.

What you'll find here

  • E-Collar Basics — how the tools work, what to buy, and how to introduce one safely.
  • Find a Trainer — a map and contact list of trainers who can help in person.
  • Intro Lessons — leash pressure, engagement, and your dog's first e-collar sessions.
  • Intermediate Lessons — adding distractions, distance, and duration.
  • Advanced Lessons — off-leash reliability and real-world proofing.
