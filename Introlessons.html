Intro Lessons

Level 1 is all about building the foundation before the e-collar is even involved: a dog that already knows the words, and already checks in with you, is far easier to teach at a distance later.

This week's lessons

Lesson 1: Loose-leash engagement

Ten minutes a day, no gadgets required. Reward your dog for choosing to walk near you instead of pulling toward everything else.

Lesson 2: Rock-solid recall word

Pick one word — I use "here" — and only ever pair it with something good, indoors, before you touch the collar at all.

Lesson 3: First collar sessions

Find your dog's working level indoors and pair a light nick with the recall word your dog already knows.

This week's checklist

  1. Practice loose-leash walking in the living room, five short sessions.
  2. Drill the recall word with treats, no leash, no collar.
  3. Fit the e-collar and find the working level (see E-Collar Basics).
  4. Run three short pairing sessions indoors.

Watch and learn

For a good visual reference on reading your dog's body language during early sessions, the ASPCA's guide to dog behavior is a solid, non-brand-specific starting point.

Comfortable with all three lessons? Move on to Intermediate Lessons.
